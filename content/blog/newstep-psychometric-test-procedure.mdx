Guidelines for Completing the Psychometric Assessment

Step 1: Type newstep.edumilestones.com in your web browser.

Step 2: Click on the "Student Dashboard" option.

Step 3: Fill in the details asked, such as your Name, E-mail ID, Password, Age, and other required information.

Step 4: You will be asked, "What are you doing now?" Please answer according to your current academic qualification. For example:

If you have passed Class 12 and are seeking guidance for graduation or technical courses, write "12th Passed."

If you have passed Class 10 and are seeking guidance for Class 11/12 stream selection, write "10th Passed."

This helps ensure that the assessment is conducted according to your present level of expertise.

Step 5: After registration, a series of assessment questions will appear. Answer all the questions carefully. Some sections are time-bound, while others have no time limit. It is advisable to complete the entire assessment in one sitting/session.

Step 6: After answering all the questions, click on the "Submit" button.

Step 7: Pay the requisite assessment fee as communicated by the counselling office. Send the screenshot of payment through whatsapp to- 9937054969/9040501588

Step 8: Your Comprehensive Psychometric Report will generally be generated within 24 hours and will be sent to your registered E-mail ID by the counsellor.

Step 9: After receiving the report, contact the counsellor and schedule an appointment for a detailed analysis and interpretation of the report.
