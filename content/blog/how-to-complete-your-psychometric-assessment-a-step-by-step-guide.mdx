A Psychometric Assessment is designed to help students gain valuable insights into their aptitude, interests, personality traits, and career suitability. Follow the steps below to complete your assessment successfully.

\## Step 1: Visit the Assessment Portal

Open your preferred web browser and navigate to:

\*\*newstep.edumilestones.com\*\*

\## Step 2: Access the Student Dashboard

On the homepage, click on the \*\*Student Dashboard\*\* option to begin the registration process.

\## Step 3: Complete Your Registration

Fill in all the required details, including:

\- Full Name

\- Email Address

\- Password

\- Age

\- Other requested information

Ensure that the information entered is accurate, as it will be used for generating your assessment report.

\## Step 4: Select Your Current Academic Status

During registration, you will be asked:

\> \*\*"What are you doing now?"\*\*

Please select or enter your current academic qualification accurately.

For example:

\- If you have completed Class 12 and are seeking guidance for graduation or technical courses, enter \*\*"12th Passed."\*\*

\- If you have completed Class 10 and are looking for guidance regarding stream selection for Classes 11 and 12, enter \*\*"10th Passed."\*\*

Providing the correct academic status helps ensure that the assessment is aligned with your educational level and future goals.

\## Step 5: Complete the Assessment

After registration, you will be presented with a series of assessment questions.

Keep the following points in mind:

\- Answer all questions honestly and thoughtfully.

\- Some sections may be time-bound, while others may have no time limit.

\- For the best experience and most accurate results, it is recommended that you complete the entire assessment in a single sitting.

\## Step 6: Submit Your Responses

Once all sections have been completed, review your responses and click the \*\*Submit\*\* button.

\## Step 7: Complete the Payment Process

Pay the assessment fee as communicated by the counselling office.

After making the payment, send a screenshot of the transaction via WhatsApp to either of the following numbers:

\- 9937054969

\- 9040501588

\## Step 8: Receive Your Psychometric Report

Your \*\*Comprehensive Psychometric Report\*\* is typically generated within \*\*24 hours\*\* of completing the assessment and payment process.

The report will be sent to your registered email address by the counsellor.

\## Step 9: Schedule a Counselling Session

After receiving your report, contact the counsellor to schedule an appointment.

During the session, the counsellor will provide a detailed analysis of your results and help you understand:

\- Your strengths and areas of potential

\- Suitable academic pathways

\- Career recommendations

\- Future educational opportunities
