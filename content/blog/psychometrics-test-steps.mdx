Step-1- Type “newstep.edumilestones.com”

Step-2- Click upon “Student Dashboard”

Step-3- Fill the questions has been asked i.e. Name, E-mail-id, Password, age, etc.

Step-4- A question will be asked “What you are doing now ?". Fill it by what you are doing currently, e.g., Suppose you are interested for Graduation or technical, and you have passed 12th standard, then write 12th passed . If you are interested for 12th standard,and passed 10th standard, then write 10th passed., because test will happen with your current expertise.

Step-5- After that a set of questions will come and answer the questions. Some questions are time bound and some are no bound of time. Try to finish all the questions in one session.

Step-6- After completion of the answering, press "submit".

Step-7- Pay the requisite amount told by the counseling office 

Step-8- Your Comprehensive psychometric report may generate within 24 hours. and will send  to your mail-id. by the counselor.

Step-9- Call the Counselor for an appointment to analyze  the report.
