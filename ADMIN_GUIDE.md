# Newstep Careers — CMS Admin Guide

This guide explains how to manage your website content using the Tina CMS admin panel.

---

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Open your browser and go to: **`https://newstepcareers.com/admin`**
2. You'll see a login page asking you to sign in with GitHub
3. Click **"Sign in with GitHub"** — use your GitHub account credentials
4. After signing in, you'll see the Tina CMS dashboard

> **Tip:** Bookmark this URL for quick access

---

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your content collections on the left sidebar:

- **Blog Posts** — Career advice articles
- **Gallery Images** — Event and workshop photos
- **Team Members** — Staff/team profiles
- **Site Settings** — Phone, email, address, social links
- **Testimonials** — Student/parent reviews
- **About Us** — Your bio, photo, mission statement

Click any collection to view and edit its content.

---

## How to Write a New Blog Post

1. Click **"Blog Posts"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button (top right, next to "Blog Posts")
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — The headline of your article
   - **Excerpt** — A short summary (1-2 sentences)
   - **Publish Date** — Today's date
   - **Category** — Select from the dropdown:
     - School Counselling
     - College Admissions
     - Career Assessments
     - Interview Tips
     - Resume & LinkedIn
     - Study Abroad
   - **Thumbnail** — Click the image icon to upload a cover photo
   - **Author** — Your name
   - **Published** — Toggle to **ON** to make it live
4. Write your article content in the large text area
5. Click **Save** (top right)

Your article will appear on the Blog page within a few minutes after the site rebuilds.

---

## How to Add Gallery Photos

1. Click **"Gallery Images"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button to add a new photo
3. Fill in:
   - **Image** — Click to upload a photo from your computer
   - **Image Description** — Describe what's in the photo
   - **Category** — Select: Events, Workshops, Achievements, or Campus
4. Click **Save**

The gallery page will update automatically after the next build.

---

## How to Update Contact Information

1. Click **"Site Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Edit any field:
   - **Hero Tagline** — The main headline on the homepage
   - **Hero Subtext** — The paragraph below the headline
   - **Phone Number** — Your contact phone
   - **Email Address** — Your business email
   - **WhatsApp Number** — Your WhatsApp (include country code, e.g., 919876543210)
   - **Address** — Your office address
   - **Google Maps Embed URL** — Your map location link
3. Update your social media links:
   - Facebook URL
   - Instagram URL
   - LinkedIn URL
   - YouTube URL
4. Click **Save**

---

## How to Update About Page Content

1. Click **"About Us"** in the sidebar
2. Edit your information:
   - **Counsellor Name** — Your full name
   - **Title / Designation** — Your professional title
   - **Profile Photo** — Upload a professional photo of yourself
   - **Bio** — Write your professional background and experience
   - **Mission Statement** — Your mission in your own words
3. Edit the timeline (your career milestones):
   - Add the year, title, and description for each achievement
4. Click **Save**

---

## How to Add or Edit Testimonials

1. Click **"Testimonials"** in the sidebar
2. To add new: Click the **+** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Student/Parent Name** — The person's name
   - **Role / College** — Their role or institution
   - **Testimonial Quote** — What they said about your service
   - **Star Rating** — 1 to 5 stars
   - **Photo** — Their photo (optional)
4. Click **Save**

---

## How to Manage Team Members

1. Click **"Team Members"** in the sidebar
2. Click **+** to add a new team member
3. Fill in:
   - **Full Name** — Team member's name
   - **Role / Title** — Their job title
   - **Team Category** — E.g., Counsellor, Advisor, Support
   - **Headshot** — Their photo
   - **Display Order** — Lower numbers appear first (1 = first)
4. Click **Save**

---

## What Happens After You Save?

When you click **Save** in Tina CMS:

1. Your changes are saved and committed to GitHub
2. Netlify automatically starts rebuilding your website
3. After 1-2 minutes, the live site updates with your changes

You'll see a "Saved" confirmation message in the CMS.

## Troubleshooting

### "Something looks wrong on the website"

1. First, try refreshing the page — sometimes it just needs a moment to update
2. If the issue persists, check if you accidentally left the **Published** toggle OFF for a blog post
3. Contact the developer with a screenshot of the issue and the URL where you see it

### "I can't log in to the admin panel"

- Make sure you're using the correct GitHub account
- If you've forgotten your GitHub password, reset it at github.com
- Contact the developer if you need a new invitation

### "My changes aren't showing up"

- Wait 2-3 minutes for the build to complete
- Check the Netlify deploys page: look for your site on Netlify and check if it's still building
- If the build fails, you'll see an error — contact the developer with the error message

### "I accidentally deleted something"

- All changes are tracked in GitHub — nothing is permanently lost
- Contact the developer and they can restore the deleted content from GitHub
